About us

Leo Cargo Packers and Movers is one of the best packaging and relocating firm in the country. We are one of the most efficient and reliable packing and logistics service providers that provide the best of the services at the least amount of time and at the most affordable cost. We offer end to end relocation services that would meet all your expectations. With years of experience in providing high quality packing and moving services, we have managed to provide hassle free experience for our clients.

We have offices in almost all major cities, which is why we can offer efficient state of the art logistics support to our services. We offer relocation services to almost all the types of clients from residential to corporate. We have trained our clients, and developed our work methodology in a way that we can provide some of the best services that would leave our clients wowed. Our major concern is keeping the goods that are entrusted with us safe and in the present condition.
