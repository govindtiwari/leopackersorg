Welcome to Leo Cargo Packers

Based in Bangalore, Leo Cargo Packers offer all types of Shifting Services like Home Shifting, Office Moving, Car Transportation, Loading Unloading of Goods, Transportation and Warehousing Service. We have our other branches in cities like Mumbai, Delhi/ Gurgaon, Chennai, Calcutta, Hyderabad, Pune, Nagpur and Surat.

With our great expertise and dedication towards our job, we apply the most advanced technology equipments to deliver the quality enhanced services. All your goods are packed in the durable quality packaging materials such as wooden crates, plywood boxes, metal or plastic drums etc. Along with this, we load and unload your goods while using the advanced equipments such as trailers, lifters, hydraulic elevators and so on.

An ISO 9001 : 2008 Certified Company
